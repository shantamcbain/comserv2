    Wine: 560ml (1pt) Elder flower-heads (tightly packed into the measure) 2 lemons 4.5l (1gal) boiling water 1.4 kg. (3lb) sugar 15g (a-oz) champagne yeast activated two days before being added.

    Put the flower-heads and thinly peeled lemon rinds into a large bowl. Pour on the boiling water and stir. Cover and leave for three days, stirring occasionally. Stir and bring to the boil, adding the sugar. Simmer for about 10 min. Allow to cool until tepid. Decant into a Primary fermentation container. Add the lemon juice and yeast. Stir to mix. Transfer under fermentation lock when rapid fermentation is complete, 2 - 3 weeks depending on temp. Bottle after four months, tying corks down. Summer drink: soak flowers overnight in lemon juice.
